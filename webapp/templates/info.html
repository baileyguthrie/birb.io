Birb.io performs a search on Twitter and analyzes the emotions of the results

Due to the limitations of Twitter's search function, this is not meant to be a comprehensive anaylsis of Twitter's emotions about a topic, this is merely an analysis of the tweets that result from a quick search of a given phrase

This means that results from a search can vary a lot over time, even from minute to minute in the case of some phrases

Uses VADER Sentiment Analyzer for text analysis

Uses icons from Feather

Charts made with Chart.js
